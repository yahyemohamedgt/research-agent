Research Agent

Audience + research question in, community signal and a creative brief out.
One messy sentence is fine — e.g. "are new parents doing sleep training anxious about doing it wrong". Leave this blank to fill in Audience and Research question separately below instead.
Name a specific identity or community, not a broad demographic — the agent searches for where that group already talks to each other (e.g. "new parents doing sleep training" beats "parents").
Phrase it as a specific claim the collected posts can prove true or false — the agent reports GREEN if the community confirms it, RED if it doesn't. A broad topic can't be confirmed or killed, so it can't get a signal (e.g. "are they anxious about being judged for going back to work after leave?" works; "thoughts on parenting" doesn't).
